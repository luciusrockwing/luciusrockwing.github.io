Market Fit

Why Real Estate Works

The core problem you're solving

A Phuket agency gets 40–80 LINE inquiries a week. Their agent answers the same 6 questions on repeat — budget, location, type, timeline, viewing, price. That's 8–15 hours of copy-paste work before a single real conversation happens. You eliminate that entirely.

High ticket value

One condo sale = 50K–500K THB commission. Agent ROI on your service is visible within the first deal.

Repetitive early questions

90% of incoming messages are qualification-stage. Perfect for automation — zero nuance required.

After-hours volume

Foreign buyers (EU, AU) message at 11pm–2am Thai time. No one is answering. Agent is.

Multi-language gap

Most agencies have one bilingual agent. Bot handles Thai, English, and basic Russian without extra hire.

Phuket-specific context

High season (Nov–Apr) floods small agencies with leads they can't process fast enough. Low season, they're chasing cold leads manually. Your agent runs 24/7 regardless — capturing the peak overflow and re-engaging dormant leads in off-season. Frame it as leveling their capacity without headcount.

System Architecture

The Full Flow

01
Inquiry Arrives
LINE OA message or web widget. Agent responds in <5 seconds, 24/7. First reply: detect language (Thai / EN / RU), acknowledge, open qualification.
02
Qualification Loop
5 structured questions delivered conversationally. Budget → Location → Property type → Bedrooms → Timeline. If user drops off mid-flow, re-engage once after 2 hours.
03
Lead Scoring
System scores the lead A/B/C based on budget range, timeline urgency, and response completeness. Score determines routing priority.
04
Property Matching
Agent queries live property sheet. Returns max 3 matching units with key specs. Offers photos (link) or viewing booking.
05
Booking & Handoff
Slot selection → calendar block → agent notification via LINE Notify. Human agent gets: name, contact, score, matched property, budget confirmed, timeline.
06
Post-Viewing Follow-up
T+24hrs: automated check-in message. T+72hrs: if no response from human agent, flag in lead log. Keeps pipeline visible.

Design principle

Every objection response offers a micro-commitment — something small and useful (digest, PDF, call) that keeps the lead warm without pressure. Never a dead-end reply.

Lead Intelligence

Lead Scoring System

Agent scores every lead before a human sees them. Agents see Grade + summary. No more "is this a real buyer" guesswork at 8am.

A
Hot — Priority Response
QUALIFIES IF
  • Budget confirmed ≥ 3M THB
  • Timeline: within 3 months
  • Specific property / area preference
  • Agreed to viewing OR callback
  • Responded to all 5 questions
AGENT ACTION

Notify agent immediately via LINE. Flag in CRM as Priority. Human must respond within 2 hours.

B
Warm — Follow Up This Week
QUALIFIES IF
  • Budget stated but vague range
  • Timeline 3–6 months
  • Has preferences but open to options
  • Dropped off after 3+ questions
AGENT ACTION

Enter into weekly nurture sequence. Agent reviews within 48 hours. Re-engage with new listings when available.

C
Cold — Nurture Sequence
QUALIFIES IF
  • Budget not confirmed or very low
  • "Just looking" / no timeline
  • Incomplete qualification (<3 questions)
  • No callback or viewing intent
AGENT ACTION

Add to monthly newsletter. No agent time spent. Re-qualify if they re-engage organically.

Sales Framing

How to Sell This

The one-liner that closes

"One qualified lead in Phuket real estate is worth 50,000–500,000 THB in commission. This system costs 15,000 THB a month. You need it to pay for itself once a quarter. The math is not complicated."

Objection handling — client side
"We already have a LINE OA"
Good — we integrate with it. No migration, no new account. We just make it intelligent.
"Our agents prefer personal contact"
They'll still do the personal contact — just on pre-qualified leads who already know what they want and when they want to view. No more 11pm "how much condo" messages.
"What if the AI says something wrong?"
It only communicates from your property sheet and your approved scripts. It doesn't speculate. Any question it can't answer, it says "let me connect you with our team".
"We don't have a property database"
Google Sheet with 5 columns is enough to start. We build the sheet structure for you in the setup. Most agencies can fill it in 2 hours.
"Can we try before we commit?"
Yes — we offer a 2-week paid pilot on the Starter tier. You see the leads coming in, you decide. No annual contract required.
Who to target first

Mid-size Phuket agencies (5–15 listings, 2–4 agents) with active LINE OA but no automation. They're big enough to have lead volume problems, small enough to not have IT teams. Check their LINE OA — if response time is slow or replies are inconsistent, that's your in. Facebook and Google reviews mentioning "hard to contact" or "slow response" are buying signals.
