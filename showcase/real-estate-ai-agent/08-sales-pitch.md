## Objection Handling — Client Side

### "We already have a LINE OA"

Good, we integrate with it. No migration, no new account. We just make it intelligent.

### "Our agents prefer personal contact"

They'll still do the personal contact. Just on pre-qualified leads who already know what they want and when they want to view. No more 11pm "how much condo" messages.

### "What if the AI says something wrong?"

It only communicates from your property sheet and your approved scripts. It doesn't speculate. Any question it can't answer, it says "let me connect you with our team".

### "We don't have a property database"

Google Sheet with 5 columns is enough to start. We build the sheet structure for you in the setup. Most agencies can fill it in 2 hours.

### "Can we try before we commit?"

Yes. We offer a 2-week paid pilot on the Starter tier. You see the leads coming in, you decide. No annual contract required.

## Who to Target First

Mid-size Phuket agencies (5–15 listings, 2–4 agents) with active LINE OA but no automation.
They're big enough to have lead volume problems, small enough to not have IT teams.
Check their LINE OA. If response time is slow or replies are inconsistent, that's your in.
Facebook and Google reviews mentioning "hard to contact" or "slow response" are buying signals.
