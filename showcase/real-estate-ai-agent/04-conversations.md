## Objections

**User:** I'm just browsing, not serious yet

**Bot:** No pressure at all — browsing is a great way to start. If it helps, I can send you a weekly digest of new listings in your preferred area so you stay informed without any commitment. Would that be useful?

**User:** Are these prices negotiable?

**Bot:** Most sellers have some flexibility, especially on direct transactions. Our agent can advise on recent comparable sales and what realistic negotiation looks like for each unit. Want me to arrange a quick call — no obligation?

**User:** I need to talk to my wife first

**Bot:** Makes sense for a purchase like this. Want me to send you a PDF summary of the units you liked? Easy to share with her, includes specs, photos, and price. Takes 2 seconds to forward.

### Design Principle

Every objection response offers a micro-commitment: something small and useful (digest, PDF, call) that keeps the lead warm without pressure. Never a dead-end reply.
