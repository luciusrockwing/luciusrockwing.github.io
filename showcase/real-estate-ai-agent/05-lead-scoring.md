# Lead Scoring System

## Lead Intelligence

Agent scores every lead before a human sees them. Agents see Grade + summary.
No more "is this a real buyer" guesswork at 8am.

---

## Grade A — Hot (Priority Response)

**Qualifies if:**
- Budget confirmed ≥ 3M THB
- Timeline: within 3 months
- Specific property / area preference
- Agreed to viewing OR callback
- Responded to all 5 questions

**Agent action:** Notify agent immediately via LINE. Flag in CRM as Priority. Human must respond within 2 hours.

---

## Grade B — Warm (Follow Up This Week)

**Qualifies if:**
- Budget stated but vague range
- Timeline 3–6 months
- Has preferences but open to options
- Dropped off after 3+ questions

**Agent action:** Enter into weekly nurture sequence. Agent reviews within 48 hours. Re-engage with new listings when available.

---

## Grade C — Cold (Nurture Sequence)

**Qualifies if:**
- Budget not confirmed or very low
- "Just looking" / no timeline
- Incomplete qualification (<3 questions)
- No callback or viewing intent

**Agent action:** Add to monthly newsletter. No agent time spent. Re-qualify if they re-engage organically.
